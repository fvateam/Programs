III / The Method

The Bach Performance System.

A twelve-month arc, run in four phases. You start with the 90-day Metabolic Reboot and build from there.

01

Reboot

First 90 days

Reset the metabolism and the habits. We pull your baseline, run the panel, and go after the one constraint actually holding you back. Fast, visible wins that make everything after this stick.

02

Priming

Build the engine

Strength, work capacity, and the base that lets you eat more and stay lean. This is the phase most programs skip, and the reason most people stall at month four.

03

Power

Add the muscle

Heavy-Light training built around your schedule. Strength and size where it actually shows, on three to four sessions a week, not six.

04

Live Lean

Make it the baseline

Lock it in for good. The body becomes the default, not the project you restart every January. This is the phase almost nobody gets to because almost nobody stays long enough.
